How It Works

01

Buy Your Form

Visit the info center. Choose your zone, pay, and receive a physical form with your unique access code printed on it.

02

Register Online

Scan the QR on your form or visit this page. Enter your code, fill in your details, and your seat is instantly reserved.

03

Show Up & Check In

Arrive at the venue. Staff scans your code at the entrance. Your seat number is confirmed and you're in.

04

Find Your Seat & Vibe

Head straight to your assigned seat — no confusion, no scrambling. Just settle in and enjoy the night.
